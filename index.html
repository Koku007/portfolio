About Me

Hello! I'm Aadi, a passionate game designer currently in my final year of a degree in Game Design and Development. Like many in this field, I fell in love with games at a young age and have been driven to turn that passion into a career ever since.

My main focus is on creating compelling ideas, narratives, and concepts. I have a strong foundation in level design and a keen analytical eye for deconstructing the games I play—I love to understand their mechanics, analyze what makes them work, and use that knowledge to elevate my own projects.

Beyond design, I'm also a skilled 2D and 3D artist with a decent knowledge of programming. I thrive on solving complex problems and collaborating with creative teams to bring unique ideas to life.
